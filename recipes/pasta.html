Creamy Tomato Pasta

Desctiption

Creamy tomato pasta with vodka, often known as "Penne alla Vodka" or "Vodka Sauce Pasta," is a delicious and indulgent Italian-American pasta dish. It's made by combining a rich and creamy tomato sauce with a splash of vodka. The vodka serves to enhance the flavor of the tomatoes and adds a unique dimension to the sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound dry short pasta, such as penne
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Pinch red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more as needed
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more as needed
  • (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps:

  • Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a large, high-sided sauté pan over medium heat until shimmering.
    2. Add the onion and sauté until softened and translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.
    3. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes, if using, and sauté until fragrant, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
    4. Carefully pour in the crushed tomatoes.
    5. Add the salt and pepper and stir to combine.
    6. Bring to a simmer and cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes.
    7. Reduce heat to low and stir in the cream.
  • Pasta
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Add the pasta and cook al dente, about 10 minutes or according to package instructions.
  • Combine
    1. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.
    2. Toss gently to combine.
    3. Add the basil, toss once more, and serve immediately with grated cheese.
